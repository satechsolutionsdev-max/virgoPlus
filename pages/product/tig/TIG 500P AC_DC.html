Product Overview

The Virgo Plus TIG 500P AC/DC is a multi-purpose, heavy-duty AC/DC Pulse TIG welder built with the latest IGBT module inverter technology and a digital control system. It can be used for DC pulse chlorine Arc welding, DC chlorine Arc welding, AC pulse chlorine Arc welding, AC chlorine Arc welding, and Manual Arc welding, making it one of the most versatile machines in the Virgo Plus range.

Designed with a user-friendly front panel for quicker parameter adjustment, this 500A machine includes an inbuilt water cooling unit to prevent overheating during continuous heavy welding. Clean welding is adjustable under AC aluminium mode, suitable for aluminium, alloy aluminium, stainless steel, copper and other non-ferrous metals across industries such as construction, logistics, chemical, and medical science.

Key Advantages

Multi-Process Welding

Supports DC pulse, DC, AC pulse, and AC TIG welding as well as Manual Arc welding – one machine for a complete range of welding processes.

Advanced Pulse Control

Slow current rise, slow drop off, low and high pulse frequency (0.2–100 Hz), and delayed gas cutoff for superior automatic and manual welding control.

Water Cooled for Heavy Duty

Inbuilt water cooling unit ensures continuous welding on heavy applications without overheating, backed by a 100% rated duty cycle.

Comprehensive Protection

Good protection against under voltage, over voltage and over current keeps the machine safe and reliable in all working conditions.
