Product Overview

The Virgo Plus TIG 630IJ is a heavy-duty DC TIG/MMA welder built around a high-grade IGBT MODULE core device and a nano-microcrystalline magnetic ring transformer. Designed for medium and heavy fabrication environments, this machine delivers excellent arcing with stable arc performance, capable of starting at as low as 10A, making it suitable for tube mills, pipes, high pressure vessels, and structural construction projects.

The TIG 630IJ features 2T/4T working mode, independently adjustable upslope and downslope, and a VRD provision for added safety during non-welding periods. Its remote control function allows weld parameters to be adjusted on the fly, reducing downtime and rework on demanding production jobs.

Key Advantages
