Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 200P AC/DC to achieve excellent welding results using advanced MOSFET inverter technology. This professional-grade AC/DC TIG welder features reverse pulse width cleaning and unique AC square wave technology, making it perfect for aluminum and aluminum alloy welding.

Built with over current, over heat, over voltage and low voltage alarm protection circuits, the TIG 200P AC/DC is safe and reliable during operation. With 2T/4T working mode and adjustable pulse width, up slope and down slope functions, it offers smart and versatile welding control.
