Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this ARC welding machine to achieve excellent welding results using advanced IGBT module inverter technology. The ARC 400 CI2 is a professional-grade 3-phase industrial welder with LCD display for precise weld management and monitoring.

This machine adopts the latest inverter technique with pulse width technology, ensuring high working efficiency while maintaining a compact and lightweight design. The full wave with full control Bridge delivers pure DC wave output resulting in exceptional welding quality across a wide range of applications.
