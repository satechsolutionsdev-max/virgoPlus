Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

All Virgo Plus welding machines come with a standard 12-month warranty from the date of purchase. Consumable parts and accessories have a 6-month warranty period. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and component failures under normal operating conditions.

You can register your product online through the warranty registration form on this page. You'll need your product serial number, purchase date, and invoice details. Alternatively, you can register through your authorized dealer at the time of purchase.

We have an extensive network of authorized service centers across India. Visit our "Our Network" page to find the nearest service center in your area. You can also submit a service request online, and our team will assist you.

Yes, we maintain adequate stock of genuine spare parts at our service centers and authorized dealers. Common spare parts are usually available for immediate replacement. For specific parts, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days.

Our machines are available in both single-phase (230V) and three-phase (400V) configurations. The specific power requirements vary by model and are clearly mentioned in the product specifications. Our technical team can help you choose the right model for your power supply.

Yes, we provide basic operational training and safety guidelines with every purchase. For institutional buyers or bulk orders, we can arrange comprehensive training sessions. Contact our sales team for details on training programs.

We're always looking for committed partners to expand our network. Visit the "Our Network" page and fill out the dealership application form. Our business development team will review your application and contact you with further details.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, cheques, and digital payments. For dealer purchases, we offer credit terms based on agreement. Contact our sales team for specific payment arrangements.
