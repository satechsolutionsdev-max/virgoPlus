About Virgo Plus

Your trusted partner in welding technology

Leading Welding Equipment Manufacturer

Virgo Solutions India LLP, operating under the brand name Virgo Plus, is a premier manufacturer of high-quality welding machines and cutting equipment. Established with a vision to provide world-class welding solutions to Indian industries, we have grown to become a trusted name in the welding equipment sector.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced technology and staffed by experienced professionals who are committed to producing equipment that meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

We specialize in a comprehensive range of welding and cutting solutions including Arc Welding, TIG Welding, MIG Welding, Submerged Arc Welding, Plasma Cutting, and Stud Welding equipment. Each product is designed with precision engineering and undergoes rigorous quality testing before reaching our customers.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred and trusted brand in welding technology, recognized globally for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to set new benchmarks in the welding equipment industry through continuous improvement and technological advancement.

Our Mission

To manufacture world-class welding and cutting equipment that empowers industries to achieve superior results. We are committed to delivering innovative solutions, exceptional quality, and outstanding service while maintaining competitive pricing and environmental responsibility.
