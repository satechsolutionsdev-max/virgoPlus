Product Overview

The Virgo Plus CUT 100M adopts advanced IGBT inverter technology for accurate feedback control with stable current output. It is a multifunction machine that can be used for both cutting and ARC welding. Exact digital current preset makes the machine easy to operate, while the Untouched Pilot Arc Start System delivers smooth arc-striking with HF Control. The pressure regulating valve does water-proof automatically and the gas meter is moulded together with the machine — no need to install.

With 2T/4T working mode for convenient operation and quality cutting thickness — fast cutting speed, smooth cutting, small deformation and less hang slag. The advanced airflow control system saves 30% air compared to traditional machines, prolonging the life of the air compressor. Energy saving and more efficient, it can cut carbon steel, stainless steel, galvanized steel, copper, aluminum and other metal sheets.
