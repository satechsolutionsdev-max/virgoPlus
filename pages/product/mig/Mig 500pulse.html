Product Overview

Virgo Plus has engineered the MIG 500 Pulse as a premium all-in-one digital pulse MIG welder that implements the overfine welding of dorplets. In the special control mode, no-splash welding is achieved for exceptionally clean results. The double pulse function delivers beautiful fish-scale weld appearance, while the digital communication interface of the domestic general standard enables seamless iteration with robots for automated production lines.

The MIG 500 Pulse features comprehensive safety coverage including flow, overpressure, undervoltage, heat protection and self protection. With the ability to save up to 100 sets of custom parameter specifications for easy calls, it greatly enhances operational efficiency. Equipped with a water-cooled/air-cooled welding gun option, 4-roll wire feeder, and a Push/Push-Pull wire feeding system — the MIG 500 Pulse is built for precision, high-output industrial and robotic welding applications.
