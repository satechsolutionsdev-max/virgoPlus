Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this Hand Laser Welding Machine to deliver professional welding results with minimal effort. The machine supports various welding modes including point, horizontal bar, hollow circle, concentric circle triangle, and double triangle, with the width of welding seam able to reach 5mm.

The system monitors the running status from time to time, monitoring and collecting the running status of lasers, chillers, and control boards. It perfectly replaces traditional arc welding and electric welding for thin stainless steel, iron plate, aluminum plate, galvanized plate, and other metal materials. The fully sealed structure has built-in water and air channels with strong anti-interference ability.
