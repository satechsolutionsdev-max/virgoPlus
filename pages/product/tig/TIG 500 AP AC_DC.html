Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 500 AP AC/DC as the ultimate high-capacity multi-purpose AC/DC pulse TIG welder using the latest IGBT module inverter technology. This 3-phase professional welder supports DC pulse chlorine arc, DC chlorine arc, AC pulse chlorine arc, AC chlorine arc and Manual Arc welding with an output current range of 20-500A.

The user-friendly front panel layout is designed in accordance with operating process principles for quicker parameter adjustment. With independently adjustable pre-flow, post-flow, upslope, downslope, slow current rise, slow drop off current, low and high pulse frequency and delayed gas cutoff — the TIG 500 AP AC/DC is perfectly suited for automatic welding across construction, logistics, chemical industry and medical science applications.
