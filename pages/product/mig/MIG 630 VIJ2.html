Product Overview

Virgo Plus MIG 630 VIJ2 adopts advanced IGBT inverter technology with high working frequency, high working efficiency and energy saving. Exact digital current/voltage reset makes the machine easy to operate. With good protection of under voltage, over voltage and over current, it makes the machine safe and reliable.

High voltage and slow feeding arc striking eliminates wire explosion and removes tip ball at arc ending. Constant voltage/constant current output supports MIG/CO2/MMA welding in one machine. With tri-proof design it ensures working stable and reliable in kinds of adverse environmental circumstances.
