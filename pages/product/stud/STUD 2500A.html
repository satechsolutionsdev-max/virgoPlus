Product Overview

The Virgo Plus STUD 2500A adopts digital and hard switch inverter technology — small in size, light in weight, and easy to move. The welding current and welding time are controlled by single-chip microcomputer, ensuring accurate welding heat and a high qualified rate. It has automatic compensation for power grid voltage fluctuation and a test gun function to check the welding gun action before welding.

With automatic/manual selection function suitable for special occasions such as multi-layer penetration welding, and an intuitive clear digital display of welding current and time. Mainly used for welding bolts of carbon steel, stainless steel and other materials. Widely used in steel structure construction, bridges, locomotives, shipbuilding, boilers, industrial furnaces and other fields.
