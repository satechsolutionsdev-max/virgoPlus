Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the MIG 250WT with single-tube IGBT high-frequency inverter technology for large, stable and reliable output power. With wire detection function (dry extension length 20mm), arc hardness adjustment, and application of leading anti-splash control circuit, it delivers small spatter, uniform shape, and beautiful appearance with good arc ignition reliability.

The MIG 250WT supports 220V/415V power input automatically for 1/2/3 PH use. Its inbuilt wire feeder is suitable for 15kg spool, enabling gas less MIG/CO2 welding with flux-core wire. Stable welding with fine wire and small current makes it especially suitable for thin plate welding down to 0.5mm without breakdown.
