Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this ARC welding machine to achieve excellent welding results using advanced IGBT inverter technology. The ARC 250D is a professional-grade inverter welder with dual-phase input capability for flexible power supply options.

This machine adopts advanced inverter technique and excellent imported capacity equipment, ensuring high working efficiency while maintaining a compact size and lightweight design. The soft magnetic ferrite material used in the high frequency transformer provides small wastage and high conversion rate.

Key Advantages

Dual Phase Input

Supports both 1PH AC 230V and 2PH 415V ±10% input, offering maximum flexibility across different site power supplies.

Energy Efficient

85% machine efficiency with passive power factor correction for significant energy savings and reduced operating costs.

Comprehensive Protection

VRD low voltage technology plus protection against over/under voltage & surge in input power ensures safe and reliable operation.

Arc Force & Anti-Stick

Arc force and anti-stick feature for better welding electrode burning resulting in improved welding performance capability.

Detailed Description

  • Latest inverter with pulse width technology – lightweight and compact design for easy portability and use across various welding environments
  • High frequency transformer with soft magnetic ferrite material offers minimal wastage and exceptional conversion efficiency for high performance
