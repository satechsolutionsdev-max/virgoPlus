Product Overview

The Virgo Plus CUT 40S adopts advanced IGBT inverter technology for accurate feedback control with stable current output. The Touch Start System delivers smooth arc-striking with HF control, while its quality cutting capability ensures fast cutting speed, smooth cutting, small deformation and less hang slag. The pressure regulating valve does water-proof automatically and the gas meter is moulded together with the machine — no need to install.

Auto-protection against over-voltage, over-current and over-heating ensures safe and reliable operation. Energy saving design is more efficient compared with traditional cutting machines. It can cut a variety of metal plates such as carbon steel, stainless steel, galvanized steel, copper and aluminum.
