Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 300PT to achieve excellent welding results using high-grade IGBT technology with non-crystal magnet ring. This professional DC TIG/MMA welder supports 1PH, 2PH and 3PH input, delivering a wide TIG current range of 20-300A for demanding industrial applications.

With independently adjustable pre-flow, post-flow, upslope and downslope, as well as high and low frequency pulse suitable for manual and automatic welding, the TIG 300PT provides exceptional control. VRD provision, thermostatic control and automatic over-voltage and over-current protection ensure safe and stable performance.

Key Advantages

3-Phase Input Support

Supports 1PH, 2PH and 3PH input (1P AC220V / 2P&3P AC415V ±10%) for maximum flexibility across different power supplies.

High-Grade IGBT Core

Adopting high-grade IGBT as core device with non-crystal magnet ring for superior efficiency and stable arc performance up to 300A.

Advanced Pulse Control

High and low frequency pulse frequency independently adjustable, suitable for both manual and automatic welding processes.

Comprehensive Protection

VRD provision, thermostatic control, automatic over-voltage and over-current protection with fan cooling for safe reliable operation.
