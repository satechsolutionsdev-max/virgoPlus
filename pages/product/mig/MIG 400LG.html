Product Overview

Virgo Plus has engineered the MIG 400 LG as a latest LCD digital technology MIG/MMA welder adopting advanced IGBT dual module inverter technology for high working frequency, high efficiency and energy saving. The welding arc is stable, spatter is small, the molten pool is deep, the weld shape is beautiful, and the welding efficiency is high. With good protection of under voltage, over voltage, and over current, it makes the machine safe and generator friendly.

The MIG 400 LG features a synergic welding function that automatically sets the working voltage and wire speed based on the working current for consistent, high-quality results. The spot weld function enables welding of low thickness sheet without causing holes. Wire diameter selection function makes it easier to match and adjust welding current and voltage. With its 4-roll wire feeder drive, 2T/4T working mode, arc ending working mode, and human & tri-proof design, the MIG 400 LG is built to deliver reliable performance in all kinds of adverse environmental conditions.
