Product Overview

The Virgo Plus ARC 1000 adopts IGBT module and Fast Recovery module with high-frequency inverter technology, ensuring large output power that is stable and reliable. With a massive output current range of 30–1000A and a high OCV of 85V, this machine is purpose-built for the most demanding heavy-duty welding sites including shipyards, steel structures and pressure vessels.

This machine is multifunctional and gouging-compatible — steep droop output with high-concentration arc energy supports standard MMA welding, while switching to a gouging torch enables back-gouging, bevelling and more. Dual-fan constant cooling with 4-fold protection provides continuous reliable performance even in the harshest environments.
