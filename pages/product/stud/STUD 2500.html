Product Overview

The Virgo Plus CD STUD 2500 is a Capacitor Discharge DC Stud Welder that is small in size and light in weight — easy to carry and move. It can display charging voltage with simple, intuitive operation offering advantages of express convenience, economy, and reliable quality. High instantaneous power with low power capacity requirement and extremely short discharge time of 0.001-0.003s ensures precision welding.

Good welding quality and adaptability allows it to replace riveting, drilling, manual ARC welding, resistance welding and fiber welding. Suitable for carbon steel, stainless steel, copper and other metal plates and STUDS. Applied widely in construction, shipbuilding, hardware, electrical appliances, chemical equipment and other industries. Welding quantity depends on output voltage and bolt diameter.
