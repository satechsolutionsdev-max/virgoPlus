Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this ARC welding machine to achieve excellent welding results using advanced IGBT inverter technology. The ARC 315D is a professional-grade inverter welder supporting both 1PH 230V and 2PH 415V input, offering a wide welding current range of 20–315A for demanding industrial applications.

This machine adopts advanced inverter technique and excellent imported capacity equipment, ensuring high working efficiency while maintaining a compact size and lightweight design. The soft magnetic ferrite material used in the high frequency transformer provides minimal wastage and high conversion rate.
