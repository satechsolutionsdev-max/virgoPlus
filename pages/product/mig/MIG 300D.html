Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the MIG 300 D with advanced SMD surface-mount design and IGBT (60K) inverter technology for high working frequency, high efficiency and energy saving. With good protection against under voltage, over voltage and over current, it makes the machine safe and reliable. Exact digital current/voltage reset ensures easy and precise operation.

The MIG 300 D supports 220V/415V power input with automatic switching for 1/2/3 PH use. Constant voltage/constant current output enables one machine to handle MIG, MMA and simple TIG welding. Soft starting and slow feeding arc striking eliminates exploding wire and removes tip ball at arc ending. With tri-proof design, it ensures stable and reliable working in all kinds of adverse circumstances.
