Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this Portable Gantry CNC Cutting Machine to deliver precise and efficient cutting results. The PG CNC 2530 offers a simple solution to precise cutting requirements and can be used with either a flame cutting (Oxy-Fuel) torch or coupled with a suitable plasma power source.

When coupled with a plasma power source, the machine is equipped with an ARC Voltage Height Controller for consistent cut quality. It features a USB interface for easy CNC code transfer and includes a standard shape library. Powered by FASTCAM software, this gantry machine supports dual drive mode and can cut directly on steel plates without occupying a fixed site.

Key Advantages

Dual Cutting Modes

Supports both Flame (Oxy-Fuel) and Plasma cutting, offering flexibility for a wide range of materials and thicknesses.

ARC Voltage Height Control

Electric Height Control combined with Arc Voltage Height Control (THC) ensures consistent cut quality during plasma operations.

High-Definition LCD Display

7-inch, 800×480 HD 16 million color LCD with 64M SDRAM and 256M electronic hard disk for reliable and smooth operation.

Dual Drive Gantry

Dual drive mode provides superior stability and accuracy during cutting operations across the full W2500 × L3000mm working area.
