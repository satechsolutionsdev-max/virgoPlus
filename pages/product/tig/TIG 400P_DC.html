Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 400P/DC to achieve excellent welding results using advanced IGBT inverter technology with high working frequency and energy saving design. This 3-phase DC TIG/MMA welder delivers outstanding performance with exact digital current and voltage preset, making it easy to operate for a wide range of professional welding applications.

The TIG 400P/DC features independently adjustable pre-flow, post-flow, upslope and downslope settings, combined with high and low frequency pulse functionality suitable for both manual and automatic welding. The high voltage arc striking allows the welding cable to be extended up to 30 meters for maximum flexibility on the job site.
