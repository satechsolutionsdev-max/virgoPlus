Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 250D to achieve excellent welding results using high-grade IGBT technology with non-crystal magnet ring. This professional DC TIG/MMA welder supports both single-phase and two-phase input, delivering reliable performance across a wide range of industrial applications.

With independently adjustable pre-flow and post-flow, 2T/4T working mode and VRD provision for safety, the TIG 250D offers smart and versatile welding control. Fan cooling, thermostatic control and automatic over-voltage and over-current protection ensure safe and consistent performance.
