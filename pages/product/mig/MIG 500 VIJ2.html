Product Overview

Virgo Plus MIG 500 VIJ2 adopts advanced IGBT dual module inverter technology with high working frequency, high working efficiency and energy saving. The product quality is reliable and performance is stable. With good protection of under voltage, over voltage, and over current, it makes the machine safe for using.

The welding arc is stable, the spatter is small, the molten pool is deep, and the weld shape is beautiful. Synergic welding function automatically sets the working voltage and wire speed based on the working current. The 4-roll wire feeder ensures no slippage in wire feeding, and the human and tri-proof design ensures stable and reliable working in adverse environmental circumstances.

Key Advantages

MIG & MMA in One

Constant voltage/constant current output characteristic — one machine supports both MIG and MMA welding for versatile applications.

Synergic Welding

Synergic welding function automatically sets working voltage and wire speed based on working current for optimal results.

Spot Weld Function

Spot weld function enables welding of low thickness sheet without causing holes, ideal for precision thin-sheet work.

4-Roll Wire Feeder

Wire feeder with 4-roll drive eliminates any chance of slippage in wire feeding for consistent, reliable performance.
