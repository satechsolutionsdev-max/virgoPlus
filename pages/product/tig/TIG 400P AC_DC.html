Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 400P AC/DC as the ultimate multi-purpose AC/DC pulse TIG welder using the latest IGBT module inverter technology with digital control system. This 3-phase professional welder supports DC pulse chlorine arc, DC chlorine arc, AC pulse chlorine arc, AC chlorine arc and Manual Arc welding, making it one of the most versatile machines in the range.

The user-friendly front panel layout is designed in accordance with operating process principles for quicker parameter adjustment. With independently adjustable pre-flow, post-flow, upslope, downslope, slow current rise, slow drop off current, low and high pulse frequency, delayed gas cutoff and arc ending adjustment — the TIG 400P AC/DC is perfectly suited for automatic welding applications.
