Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this ARC welding machine to achieve excellent welding results using advanced IGBT module inverter technology. The ARC 630GIJ is a professional-grade 3-phase inverter welder with full wave full control bridge for pure DC wave output, delivering exceptional welding performance with a wide output current range of 50–600A.

This machine features adjustable HOT start & ARC force, separate current and volt meter, VRD provision for safety during non-welding, and heavy duty lug type output terminals. The ARC 630GIJ can also be used for gouging applications, making it one of the most versatile machines in the ARC series for demanding industrial environments.
