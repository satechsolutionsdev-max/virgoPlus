Product Overview

The Virgo Plus CUT 200CNC adopts advanced IGBT inverter technology with high working frequency and high working efficiency. It features good protection against under voltage, over voltage and over current, making the machine safe and reliable. Industrial type, heavy duty design ensures easy moving. The exact digital current preset makes the machine easy to operate, and the 2T/4T working mode provides convenient operation.

The advanced air flow control system saves 30% air compared to traditional machines, prolonging the life of the air compressor. With self-lock working mode, it reduces working intensity greatly and ensures working stability in adverse circumstances. Suitable for both manual and automatic cutting (CNC compatible). Can be used for carbon steel, stainless steel, galvanised steel, copper, aluminium and other metal sheets.
