Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the PG PP CNC 1530 as a professional plate and pipe CNC cutting machine, supporting both plate and pipe cutting operations. It supports both flame cutting and plasma cutting, making it a highly versatile solution for industrial cutting applications.

The whole machine is compact in appearance, light in weight, stable in operation, and high in cutting precision. It features a large number of standard and special graphical macro libraries to choose from and is widely used in cutting carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum sheets. The machine is equipped with the Shanghai Fangling F2100BX CNC controller and F1621 THC, and is programmed using TubeMaster (2 Axis) software.
