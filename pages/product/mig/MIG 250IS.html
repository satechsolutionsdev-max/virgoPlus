Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the MIG 250 IS with advanced IGBT inverter technology and voltage-type PWM control. Due to the use of electronic reactors, the welding spatter is small and welding forming is good. The wire feeder is external and easy to move, greatly improving the working range.

With close-loop feedback, constant voltage output, and super ability of voltage anti-fluctuation, the MIG 250 IS delivers reliable performance. Digital display with welding current & voltage preset function offers a sufficient wide adjustment range. The machine supports 2T/4T working mode for convenient operation.
