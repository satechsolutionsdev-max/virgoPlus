Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 315P AC/DC to achieve excellent welding results using advanced MOSFET inverter technology with SMT surface mount design. This 3-phase AC/DC TIG welder delivers outstanding performance for both AC and DC TIG welding with pulse functionality, making it the ideal choice for aluminum welding and precision applications.

This machine features multipoint overheat protection with comprehensive safeguards against undervoltage and over current conditions. The pulse function allows the frequency, duty ratio and valley current to be adjusted freely, providing exceptional control over the welding process with 2T/4T working mode smart operation.

Key Advantages

AC/DC TIG Welding

Supports both AC and DC TIG welding modes, enabling aluminum welding and a wide range of material applications with clean, precise results.

Advanced Pulse Function

Freely adjustable frequency, duty ratio and valley current under Pulse function for superior weld quality and control.

Comprehensive Protection

Multipoint overheat protection with good protection of undervoltage and over current, ensuring safe and reliable operation.

HF Arc Starting

Instantaneous arc starting with small current welding using HF vibration technology for consistent and reliable weld initiation.
