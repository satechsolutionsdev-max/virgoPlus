Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the TIG 400IJ for heavy-duty industrial welding using high-grade IGBT MOUDLE as core device with nano-microcrystalline magnetic ring transformer. This 3-phase DC TIG/MMA welder delivers a wide TIG current range of 10-400A, making it ideal for medium and heavy fabrication, production plates, tube mills, pipes and high pressure vessels.

With arc force and hot start function for arc stability, independently adjustable upslope and downslope, 2T/4T working mode, VRD provision and remote control function to adjust weld parameters, the TIG 400IJ offers exceptional control and flexibility to significantly reduce downtime and rework on demanding industrial projects.
