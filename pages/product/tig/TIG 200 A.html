Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured this TIG welding machine to achieve excellent welding results using advanced Mosfet inverter technology. The TIG 200A is a professional-grade DC TIG/MMA welder with high frequency arc starting for easy and reliable striking performance.

This machine adopts advanced inverter technology with original imported components, ensuring high working efficiency while maintaining a compact size and lightweight design. The advanced cooling technology makes the TIG 200A more efficient and more stable than traditional machines.
