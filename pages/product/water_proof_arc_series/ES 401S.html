Product Overview

Virgo Plus has specially configured the ES 401 S as part of the ARC Water Proof Series to deliver reliable and safe MMA welding performance across single, two and three phase power inputs. With IGBT inverter technology, SMT surface mount design on PCBs and VRD protection meeting CE requirements, this machine ensures enhanced reliability and operator safety.

The ES 401 S features an advanced 30KHz high working frequency inverter technique that saves energy while maintaining stable welding performance. Its welding cable can be extended up to 100 meters, and its separated arc force knob allows for easy, precise adjustment — making it ideal for outdoor work, generator-powered sites and heavy industrial applications.

Key Advantages

1PH, 2PH & 3PH Input

Self-adaptive power switch supporting 110V–280V for single phase and 280V–560V for three phase, ensuring compatibility across a wide range of power supplies.

85% Efficiency

High efficiency operation with original imported IGBT components and advanced 30KHz inverter technique for significant energy savings.

VRD Protection

VRD protection meeting CE requirements, combined with tri-proof design for safe and reliable operation in all kinds of adverse circumstances.

Separated Arc Force Knob

Easily adjustable arc force knob for precise welding control, compatible with acid, basic and high strength electrodes up to 4.0mm.
